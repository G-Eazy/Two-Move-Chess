Double chess tutorial


Game rules

Piece movement: Piece movement is the same as in regular chess.

Turns: White begins with one move. Then black answers with two moves, followed by whites two moves. After this the game continues with two moves each turn.

End of the game: If the king is captured, the game is over.
Otherwise the game ends with a timeout, resignation or draw offer.

Other rules: There is no concept of a 'hard pin' or being in 'check' in this game.
